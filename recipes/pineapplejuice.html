Pineapple Juice

Description

Pineapple juice is a juice made from pressing the natural liquid out from the pulp of the pineapple (a fruit from a tropical plant). Numerous pineapple varieties may be used to manufacture commercial pineapple juice, the most common of which are Smooth Cayenne, Red Spanish, Queen, and Abacaxi.

Ingredients

  • Pineapple
  • Water
  • Turmeric / Black Pepper (Optional)
  • Sugar (Optional)

Steps

  1. Cut pineapple into slices
  2. Blend with water.
  3. Add spices and sugar to taste.
  4. Enjoy.
